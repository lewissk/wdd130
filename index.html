Hello! My name is Scott Lewis and I live in Herriman, Utah. I enjoy mountain biking, climbing, skiing, and camping. My parents had a small farm and I did a lot of ranching and farming with my parents and siblings. I served a mission in the Rome Italy mission where I served in Florence, Naples, Sardegna, and Rome. I married my lovely wife and sealed in the Salt Lake Temple. My wife and I have 4 children. I have been a programmer for a long time and have coded in javascript, scala, java, python, go, swift and kotlin. I love to do 3d printing and electronic circuit boards to make various things. I have lived in San Jose California, Phoenix AZ, and in many places in Utah. I have a pet golden retriever named Enzo who is two years old. Enzo loves to play fetch with a ball, go for walks, and play with his stuffed animals. I also have a 3d printer that I use to create things that I come up with using Autodesk Fusion 360. I am currently working on a project to create an Ironman costume that I will have displayed in my basement where I have my flight simulator and soon to have my full motion Formula 1 racing simulator.
